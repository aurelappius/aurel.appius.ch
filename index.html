Aurel X. Appius

I am a master's student in Robotics, Systems and Control at ETH Zurich. My main interest is in building intelligent robots that can interact with the environment and solve real world problems. At ETH, I have worked on Raptor, a quadcopter equipped with a soft gripper designed for the rapid picking up of objects. With a strong background in mathematics, physics, and computer science, I want to tackle the problems of tomorrow!
